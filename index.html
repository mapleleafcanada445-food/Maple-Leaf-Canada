Corporate Governance & Ethics

Our 100 Year History & Heritage +
Founded in 1927, Maple Leaf Foods has grown into a global leader in the food industry. Over a century, we have built a reputation for stability, quality, and integrity. Joining our team means becoming part of a legacy that feeds millions of families across the world every single day.
International Worker Rights +
We operate strictly under the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms. Every international recruit is entitled to fair pay, a safe work environment, regulated working hours, and full health insurance coverage.
Privacy & Data Security Policy +
Your personal information, including passport and identification documents, is protected using high-level AES-256 encryption. We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).
